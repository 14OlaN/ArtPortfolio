Value of Portraiture - 2016-17

Pre-Instruction

Post-Instruction
In my pre-instruction portrait, there's an attempt at creating an illusion of depth.
  • visible outlines of the face, nose, mouth, and ear
  • attempt to create soft transitions
  • lack of the full range of value
 In contrast with the pre-instruction portrait, my final portrait demonstrates all three rules of creating the illusion of depth.
  • no outlines - borders created using contrasting values
  • soft transitions - on hair, face, and neck
  • full range of value - her hair is the lightest, and the hair near her face and neck is the darkest
  • Nose: wrong shading and outlines
  • Eyes: wrong shape, no shadow on the whites, wrong shape and size of iris (too large)
  • Lips: no shading - flat
  • Nose: shaded correctly
  • Eyes: not visible on the picture but there's shadow on the whites, right shape of iris
  • Lips: three-dimensional and round
